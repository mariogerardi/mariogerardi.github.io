How to Play:

Decrypt as many puzzles as possible. For each puzzle, you are given two clues - a seven-letter word, and a hint for the answer. In the seven-letter word, a green square indicates that the letter is in the answer, in the same spot. A blue square indicates that the letter is also in the answer, but in a different spot. A gray square indicates that the letter is not in the answer.

Example: the word SPANDEX, coupled with the clue "instruction clarifier," rearranges to become EXAMPLE.

Press Begin to generate the first puzzle and start the timer. You are allotted 15 seconds to solve each puzzle. With each correct answer, the timer resets. Once the timer runs out, the game is over. Press Reset at any point to reset the game.


Decrypt is word game inspired by Josh Wardle's Wordle that incorporates clues reminscent of crossword puzzles, and a timer that keep players on their toes and rewards them for correct guesses.
