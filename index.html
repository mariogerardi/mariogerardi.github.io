DECODE

by Mario Gerardi

Time:20
Score:0

How to Play:


For each puzzle, you are given two clues - a word, and a hint for the answer. In the word, a green square indicates that the letter is in the answer, in the same spot. A blue square indicates that the letter is also in the answer, but in a different spot. A gray square indicates that the letter is not in the answer.

Example: the word EXPANSE, coupled with the clue "instruction clarifier," rearranges to become EXAMPLE.

TIMED Mode:


Ensure that TIMED is selected at the top of the page. Press Begin to generate the first puzzle and start the timer. You are allotted 20 seconds to solve each puzzle. The puzzles will become more difficult as you continue to solve them. With each correct answer, the timer resets to 20 seconds. Once the timer runs out, the game is over.



Daily 5:


Ensure that DAILY 5 is selected at the top of the page. Press Begin to generate the first puzzle. You are allotted as much time as necessary to solve each puzzle. With each correct answer, a new, harder puzzle is generated - you may not move onto the next puzzle until you have completed the previous one. After you've completed the Daily 5, the timer stops, and your score is displayed.


Decode is word game inspired by Josh Wardle's Wordle that incorporates clues reminscent of crossword puzzles.
